What is Oanda:
-"Through our integration with OANDA Brokerage, we can offer FOREX or CFD trading to users worldwide. Accounts can be opened with as little as $1 USD." // from docs v1
-Oanda was founded by Dr. Michael Stumm and Dr. Richard Olsen in 1995 with the goal to "transform all aspects of how the world interacts with currencies, whether that be trading or utilizing currency data and information". Oanada provides access to trading Forex and CFDs for clients in over 240 countries and territories with no minimum deposit [source]. Oanda also provides demo accounts, advanced charting tools, and educational content.
