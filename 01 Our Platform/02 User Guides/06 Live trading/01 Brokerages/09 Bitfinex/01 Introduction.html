What is Bitfinex:
-"As a cryptocurrency exchange founded in 2012, Bitfinex has always been at the forefront of technological innovation in digital asset trading. " // from docs v1
-Bitfinex was founded by Giancarlo Devasini and Raphael Nicolle in 2012 with the goal to "give our users the ultimate cryptocurrency trading experience" [source]. Bitfinex provides access to trading Cryptocurrencies for clients outside prohibited jurisdictions with no minimum deposit to get an account set up. When funding your account with fiat currencies there is a 10,000 minimum for USD, EUR, and GBP [source]. However, when funding your account with Cryptocurrencies, there is no minimum. Bitfinex also provides Cryptocurrency staking, a mobile app, and an unrealized profit leaderboard for traders on the platform.

What order types are supported:
-market, limit, stop market, stop limit, market on open, market on close, limit if touched

What asset classes are supported:
-Crypto

What leverage is available:
-Cash accounts: 1x
-Margin accounts: 3.3x

What are the fees:
-We don't currently model 30-day volume. We model fixed maker/taker fees
-Maker fees: 0.1%
    - Maker fees are paid when you add liquidity to our order book by placing a limit order under the ticker price for buy and above the ticker price for sell.
-Taker fees: 0.2%
    - Taker fees are paid when you remove liquidity from our order book by placing any order that is executed against an order of the order book.
    - If you place a hidden order, you will always pay the taker fee. If you place a limit order that hits a hidden order, you will always pay the maker fee.
Taker fees are paid when you remove liquidity from our order book by placing any order that is executed against an order of the order book.
