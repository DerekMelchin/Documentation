The Interactive Brokers (IB) data feeds are streams of Equity, Futures, and Options prices directly from IB. The data feed is a summarized snapshot of the trades and quotes at roughly 300 milliseconds per snapshot. In contrast, our data feed consolidates the trades and quotes across all of the exchanges into a real-time stream with a 50 millisecond delay. To use IB data feeds in your algorithms, subscribe to IB market data. We support all of the IB data subscriptions that are related to the securities and markets we support. Members usually subscribe to the following IB market data:

  • US Securities Snapshot and Futures Value Bundle
  • US Equity and Options Add-On Streaming Bundle

If you deploy a live algorithm with the IB brokerage, you can use the QC data feed, the IB data feed, or both.
