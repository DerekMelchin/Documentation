Oanda supports trading Forex and CFDs.

AddForex("EURUSD", Resolution.Minute, Market.Oanda);

AddCfd("AU200AUD", Resolution.Minute, Market.Oanda);
self.AddForex("EURUSD", Resolution.Minute, Market.Oanda)

self.AddCfd("AU200AUD", Resolution.Minute, Market.Oanda)

If you call the SetBrokerageModel method with the correct BrokerageName, then you don't need to pass a Market argument to the method calls above because the brokerage model automatically selects the correct market.
