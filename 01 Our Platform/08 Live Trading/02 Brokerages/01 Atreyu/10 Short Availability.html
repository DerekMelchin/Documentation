Atreyu Trading provides short availability information through the AtreyuShortableProvider. If you try to short an Equity when there are no shares available to borrow, your order is invalidated.

var quantity = CalculateOrderQuantity(symbol, -0.1m);
var canShort = Shortable(symbol, quantity);
quantity = self.CalculateOrderQuantity(self.symbol, -0.1)
