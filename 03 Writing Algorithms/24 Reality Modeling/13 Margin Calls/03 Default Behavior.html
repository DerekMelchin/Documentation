The brokerage model of your algorithm automatically sets the margin call model for the portfolio. The default brokerage model is the DefaultBrokerageModel, which sets the DefaultMarginCallModel. The DefaultMarginCallModel issues margin call warnings when the margin remaining in your portfolio is less than or equal to 5% of the total portfolio value. When your total margin used exceeds 10% of the total portfolio value, this model creates margin call orders. It sorts the generated margin call orders in ascending order by their unrealized profit and then synchronously executes each order until your portfolio is within the margin requirements.
