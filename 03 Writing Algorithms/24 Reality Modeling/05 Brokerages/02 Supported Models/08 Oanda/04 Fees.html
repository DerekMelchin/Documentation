The OandaBrokerageModel uses the ConstantFeeModel with zero fees. OANDA offers two pricing models - "Spread" and "Core + Commission". As our data is the spread dataset we model the spread pricing. Using a custom fee model and fill model, you can estimate the OANDA Core+Commission data.

security.SetFeeModel(new ConstantFeeModel(0.0m));
security.SetFeeModel(ConstantFeeModel(0))
