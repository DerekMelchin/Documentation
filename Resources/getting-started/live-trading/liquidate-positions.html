The live results page has a Liquidate button that acts as a "kill switch" to sell all of the non-Equity holdings in your portfolio at the current market price using market orders and then stop the algorithm. For instance, if your algorithm has a bug in it that caused it to purchase a lot of securities that you didn't want, you can liquidate your portfolio instead of placing many manual trades.

Follow these steps to liquidate your positions:

  1. Open your algorithm's live results page.
  2. Click Liquidate.
  3. Click Liquidate again.
